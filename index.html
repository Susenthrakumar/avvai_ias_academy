About Avvai IAS Academy

Empowering Aspirants for a Successful Career in Civil Services

Your Journey Begins Here

Founded in 2010, Avvai IAS Academy is one of the leading institutions for civil services coaching, specializing in TNPSC, SI, and other competitive exams. With a legacy of success, we aim to nurture and guide aspiring civil servants towards achieving their goals.

Our Core Strengths

We offer a comprehensive range of services to ensure success at every step of your preparation:

Detailed Study Material

In-depth notes and resources that cover every topic in the syllabus, simplifying complex concepts for easy understanding.

Timed Mock Tests

Regular mock tests to track progress, improve speed and accuracy, and build exam confidence.

Interactive Doubt Clearing

Personalized sessions to address your doubts and reinforce your understanding of key concepts.

Expert Interview Guidance

Specialized interview preparation with mock interviews, personalized feedback, and confidence-building strategies.

Our Courses

Specialized programs designed for your success

TNPSC Exam Preparation

Comprehensive coaching for all groups of Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission examinations with specialized focus on current affairs, Tamil Nadu specific syllabus, and previous year question analysis.

Key Features:
  • Group 1, 2, 2A, and 4 specialized coaching
  • Bilingual study materials (Tamil & English)
  • Weekly tests and performance analytics
  • Interview training for shortlisted candidates

Test Series

Regular assessments to track your progress

Weekly Tests

Subject-wise Weekly Tests

Weekly subject-focused tests to strengthen your fundamentals and concept clarity. Each test is followed by detailed solutions and discussion.

  • Duration: 1 hour
  • Question type: MCQs
  • Performance analytics provided
Monthly Tests

Comprehensive Monthly Assessment

Monthly comprehensive tests covering multiple subjects to simulate exam conditions and build stamina for the actual examination.

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Question type: MCQs and descriptive
  • One-on-one feedback session
Mock Tests

Full-length Mock Examinations

Full-length mock tests designed exactly like the actual examination with the same pattern, difficulty level, and time constraints.

  • Full exam duration
  • Actual exam pattern followed
  • Detailed result analysis
Special Tests

Current Affairs Special

Special tests focused exclusively on current affairs, which form a significant portion of both TNPSC and SI examinations.

  • Bi-weekly current affairs tests
  • Tamil Nadu specific current events
  • National and international affairs

Digital Assessment Platform

  • 24/7 accessible platform
  • Instant evaluation
  • Performance comparison with peers
